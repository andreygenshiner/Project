Getting Started with
House of Hamsters

House of Hamsters is a blockchain game that rewards players
for their smart decision-making. Play Texas Hold’em and earn
cryptocurrency.

01 Register

Download the House of Hamsters game from Google Play or the App Store
on your smartphone and create an account.

02 Set Up Your Wallet

After registration, set up your wallet within the app.

03 Get a Free Hamster and Try to Play

Right after sign-up, enjoy a complimentary non-NFT character, Hamster,
and an energy boost. Now you can immerse yourself in the app, take on
energy tournaments, and challenge rivals.

04 Start making $HAMC

Secure top-three spots in energy tournaments for rewarding $HAMC token
earnings.

05 Boost Earnings with NFTs

Enhance energy tournament gains through NFT characters or attributes.

$HAMC

$HAMC is a token created on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with an
unlimited total emission, functioning as a digital asset operating on the
BSC blockchain. $HAMC serves as the key token within the house of
Hamsters ecosystem, managing the in-game economy and ensuring its
stability, enabling players to monetize their activities and play to earn.

Transparency

House of Hamsters uses the cutting-edge

fair competition.
